# MZ Travels - Travel & Tourism Website

## Overview

MZ Travels is a responsive travel and tourism website developed to showcase the services, destinations, and achievements of a travel company. The website provides information about national and international tour packages, destination galleries, company background, and contact information through a modern and user-friendly interface.

The website follows a single-page scrolling design, allowing visitors to explore all sections smoothly without navigating between multiple pages.

## Features

### Home Section

* Company introduction
* Company name and description
* Attractive landing section

### Services Section

* National Tours Service
* International Tours Service
* Interactive service buttons

### National Tours

* Grid-based card layout
* Destination images
* Tour descriptions
* Book Now buttons

### International Tours

* Grid-based card layout
* International destination listings
* Destination images
* Tour descriptions
* Book Now buttons

### Gallery Section

* Collection of destination images
* Card-based gallery design
* Visual showcase of travel experiences

### About Us Section

* Company background

* Company information

### Contact Us Section

* Contact information
* Customer communication section
* Easy accessibility for inquiries
